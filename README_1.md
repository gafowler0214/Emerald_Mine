# Sherman, TX 75092 — New Construction Communities Map

Interactive map of verified Sherman new-construction communities, prepared by **Emerald Cities Real Estate Group** as a companion to the Sherman Market Intelligence white paper on the Vance tract.

## What this is

A single-file interactive map (`index.html`) showing **16 verified active and imminently-active new-construction communities** in Sherman, Texas, with the Vance tract (Pleasant Home Rd, APN 123382) marked as the subject property.

## Methodology

Every community was independently verified through:

- The relevant **builder's primary community page** (Lennar, D.R. Horton, Highland Homes, K. Hovnanian, Meritage, Stonehollow, CastleRock, DRB, Brightland, Dunhill, Chesmar, LGI, Lillian, Astoria, Century, HistoryMaker, The New Modern Home)
- Corroborating **NewHomeSource and Zillow community listings**
- **NTREIS MLS records** for actual sale-price data over the trailing 24 months
- Cross-reference against the **City of Sherman Planned Development Districts** roster

**Tier classification** uses actual MLS-recorded median sale price where the community has sufficient transaction history. For communities too new to have MLS history, classification uses the builder's published advertised pricing as a placeholder.

**MLS data limitation:** The MLS captures only the broker-listed slice of new-construction transactions in Sherman. A meaningful share of builder-direct closes never reach the MLS. The MLS-recorded close counts shown should therefore be read as a verifiable floor, not a ceiling, on actual production volume.

**Coordinates** are estimated from confirmed builder addresses or directional descriptions. Two verified communities (The Preserve, Hanolley Hills) appear in the sidebar without map pins because their precise locations could not be confirmed to a level of accuracy worth pinning.

## Hosting on GitHub Pages

1. Create a new repository (public)
2. Upload `index.html` and `README.md` to the root
3. Go to **Settings → Pages**
4. Under **Source**, select **Deploy from a branch**
5. Choose **main** / **(root)** and Save
6. Your map will be live at `https://[username].github.io/[repo-name]/` within a few minutes

No build step. No dependencies to install. The map uses Leaflet from a CDN and OpenStreetMap tiles, which are served directly.
